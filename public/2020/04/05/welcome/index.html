Welcome

Published Sun, Apr 5, 2020 by JE

Estimated reading time: 1 min

This is an attempt to make hydrology topics and workflows available to whomever. There will be three different resources you can look at: blog, long winded, and projects. Blog is a brief rant, discussion, or workflow that will be relatively short. As you can imagine, long winded posts will be longer rants, discussions, or workflows. And, for the projects resource it will be the very tedious, dense, copious, and downright exaustive workflows. Please keep an open mind as this might get very pedantic and simpleton at times! I’m not an academic or an expert so expect a wide range of dialogue and detail within these documents. Please let me know of any comments or improvements so that we can progress within this domain!

Thanks,
